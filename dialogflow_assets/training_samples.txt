# Welcome intends:

1. Hello! How can I help you? You can say "New Order" or "Track Order"
2. Good day! What can I do for you today? You can say "New Order" or "Track Order" to place a order or track existing order.
3. Greetings! How can I assist? You can say "New Order" or "Track Order"
4. “Hi there 👋! Welcome to Hungry Panda. Would you like to place a new order or track your order today?”
5. “Hi! 👋 You can place a new order or track your order. Which one would you like to do?”
6. “Hello there! I can help you with new orders or tracking existing ones. What would you like?”




# Default fallback Intents

1. Sorry, I didn’t catch that. Would you like to place a new order or track an existing one?
2. Hmm, I’m not sure what you mean. You can say “New Order” to start ordering food or “Track Order” to check your delivery status.
3. My bad! I can help you with placing a new order or tracking your order. Which one would you like?
4. Could you rephrase that? I can help you with food delivery. Options: New Order | Track Order.
